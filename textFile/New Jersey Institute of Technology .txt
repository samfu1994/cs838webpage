<p2 New Jersey Institute of Technology p2> is a public institution that was founded in 1881. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 8,008, its setting is urban, and the campus size is 45 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. New Jersey Institute of Technology's ranking in the 2017 edition of <n Best n> Colleges is National Universities, <n 135 n> . Its in-state tuition and fees are $16,108 (2016-17); out-of-state tuition and fees are $30,326 (2016-17).
