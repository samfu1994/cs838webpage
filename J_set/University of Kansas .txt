<p2 University of Kansas p2> is a public institution that was founded in 1865. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 19,245, its setting is city, and the campus size is 1,000 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. University of Kansas's ranking in the 2017 edition of <n Best n> Colleges is National Universities, 118. Its in-state tuition and fees are $10,549 (2016-17); out-of-state tuition and fees are $25,932 (2016-17).
