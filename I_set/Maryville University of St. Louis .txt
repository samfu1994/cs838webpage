Maryville University of St. Louis is a private institution that was founded in 1872. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 2,795, its setting is suburban, and the campus size is 130 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. Maryville University of St. Louis's ranking in the 2017 edition of Best Colleges is National Universities, 164. Its tuition and fees are $27,958 (2016-17).
