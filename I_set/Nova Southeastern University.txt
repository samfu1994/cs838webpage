<p1 Nova Southeastern University p1> is a private institution that was founded in 1964. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 4,641, its setting is suburban, and the campus size is 314 acres. It utilizes a trimester-based academic calendar. Nova Southeastern University's ranking in the 2017 edition of Best Colleges is National Universities, 214. Its tuition and fees are $28,736 (2016-17).
NSU acknowledges and supports its student population's diversity. Whether a recent high school graduate or working/professional adult, <p1 NSU p1> provides its students with a rich variety of degree programs that are neither time nor place bound. Through innovative use of technology and creative classroom formats, we provide students with flexible, alternative learning contexts. NSU's <n Dual n> Admission program allows highly qualified students to apply for graduate or professional school when they apply for undergraduate admission. Our Dual Admission opportunities include law, dispute resolution, psychology, marine sciences, osteopathic medicine, optometry, pharmacy, physical therapy, occupational therapy, physician assistant, dental medicine, or speech-language <n pathology n>.
