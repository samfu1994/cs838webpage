<p2 Missouri University of Science & Technology p2> is a public institution that was founded in 1870. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 6,841, its setting is rural, and the campus size is 284 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. Missouri University of Science & Technology's ranking in the 2017 edition of Best Colleges is National Universities, 164. Its in-state tuition and fees are $9,628 (2016-17); out-of-state tuition and fees are $26,152 (2016-17).

The school has a particularly wide variety of engineering majors for undergraduates to choose from, including aerospace engineering, mining engineering, petroleum engineering and engineering management. However, undergraduate students can also opt to study humanities or several liberal arts disciplines, such as philosophy and history.
