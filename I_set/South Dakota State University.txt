<p2 South Dakota State University p2> is a public institution that was founded in 1881. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 11,007, its setting is rural, and the campus size is 261 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. South Dakota State University's ranking in the 2017 edition of Best Colleges is National Universities, 202. Its in-state tuition and fees are $8,172 (2016-17); out-of-state tuition and fees are $11,403 (2016-17).
<p2 SDSU p2> is the state's land grant institution. The University is organized into 7 colleges: Agriculture & <n Biological n> Sciences, Arts & Sciences, Education & Human Sciences, Engineering, Nursing, <n Pharmacy n>, and General Studies. The land grant mission includes three integral and interrelated parts. These include the Agricultural Experiment Station: the research piece, South Dakota State University Extension: the public service piece, and the University: the educational piece.
