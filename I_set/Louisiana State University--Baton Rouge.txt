Louisiana State University—Baton Rouge, better known as <p2 LSU p2>,  is a public school near the Mississippi River, in the southeastern part of the state. Outside the classroom, LSU students can <n explore n> more than 350 student organizations or check out the unique, free facilities nearby, including the 14-acre Hilltop Arboretum or the Louisiana Museum of Natural History.
